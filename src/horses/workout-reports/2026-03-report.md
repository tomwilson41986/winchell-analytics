## Summary

An active week of training with all three horses in the barn recording works at Oaklawn Park. Bold Command continues to work sharply following his stakes win, while Tapit's Legacy and Winchell's Pride are progressing well through their development.

## Highlights

- **Bold Command** posted a bullet work on March 20, covering 5 furlongs in 59.80 — best of 24 at the distance. Followed up with an easy 6-furlong maintenance breeze.
- **Winchell's Pride** worked 5 furlongs in company in 1:00.40, finishing up sharply. She continues to mature and is training with increased confidence following her maiden win.
- **Tapit's Legacy** recorded two works during the week, including a strong 4-furlong breeze in 48.20. He is being brought along steadily.

## Looking Ahead

Bold Command is being pointed toward a graded stakes appearance in April. Winchell's Pride may step up to allowance company in her next start. Tapit's Legacy continues to develop and is expected to make his first start within the next several weeks.
