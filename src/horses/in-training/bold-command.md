## Background

Bold Command is a dark bay colt by Into Mischief out of Graceful Belle, purchased at the 2024 OBS March Two-Year-Old Sale for $275,000. A stakes-quality runner currently competing at Oaklawn Park.

## Training Notes

A powerful colt who has shown a strong turn of foot in the stretch. His most recent stakes win at the Oaklawn Stakes demonstrated his ability to rate off the pace and finish strongly. Bullet workout on March 20 was best of 24 at the distance.
