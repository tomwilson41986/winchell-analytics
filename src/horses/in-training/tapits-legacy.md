## Background

Tapit's Legacy is a bay colt by Tapit out of Midnight Star, purchased at the 2024 Keeneland September Yearling Sale for $550,000. He is currently in training with Steve Asmussen at Oaklawn Park.

## Training Notes

Showing steady progress through early works. Has demonstrated a strong, ground-covering stride and a professional attitude at the track. Working well in company and responding to increased distances.
